Delivery and Refund Policy

Delivery Policy

At Kalena's Bakery, we deliver fresh, handcrafted treats right to your door across North Carolina!

  • Delivery Days: Tuesday to Saturday, 9 AM-6 PM.
  • Order Cutoff: Orders must be placed by 5PM the day before delivery.
  • Delivery Fee: $5-$15 based on distance (free for orders over $50)
  • Delivery Area: Deliveries can only be made to locations in NC.

Refund Policy

At Kalena's Bakery, we strive to deliver fresh, high-quality baked goods that exceed your expectations. However, we understand that issues may arise. Please review our refund policy below:

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Damaged Items: If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with a clear photo of the damaged item and the packaging.
  • Incorrect Orders: If you receive the wrong item, contact us within 48 hours with your order details and a photo of the incorrect product.
  • Quality Concerns: If you are dissatisfied with the quality of your baked goods, reach out within 48 hours of receiving your order to discuss a resolution.

Process for Refunds

  1. Submit a refund request by sending an email with your order number, description of issue, and photos (if applicable).
  2. Once your request is reviewed, you will be notified of our decision within 2-3 business days.
